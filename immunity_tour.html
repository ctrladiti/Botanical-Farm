Plants for Immunity

Discover powerful medicinal plants that boost your immune system and help fight infections. These natural remedies have been used for centuries in AYUSH systems to strengthen the body's defenses.

Strengthen Your Natural Defenses

Our immune system is our body's natural defense against pathogens. In traditional AYUSH systems, certain plants have been revered for their immune-boosting properties for thousands of years.

Did You Know?

Tulsi (Holy Basil) contains compounds that enhance the activity of immune cells and help fight infections. It's considered a sacred plant in Ayurveda for its healing properties.

These plants work through various mechanisms - some stimulate the production of white blood cells, others contain antioxidants that protect cells from damage, and some have antimicrobial properties that directly fight pathogens.
